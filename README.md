# OpenMusE – Hospitality Survey (Task 1.3)

This repository contains the research materials underlying the analysis presented in **OpenMusE Deliverable D1.3**.


---

## Repository Contents

### Machine-Readable Metadata
- `OpenMusE T1.3 Hospitality Survey Distributed Through Email full.xml`  
  Structured metadata file describing survey design, variables, coverage, and methodology.

- `OpenMusE_T1.3_Hospitality_Survey_Survey_Email_codebook_v1.0.xlsx`  
  Variable-level codebook including response categories and routing logic.

---

### Survey Data

Country-level datasets:

- `OpenMusE_T1.3_Hospitality_Survey_Survey_Email_data_HU.csv`
- `OpenMusE_T1.3_Hospitality_Survey_Survey_Email_data_HU.sav`
- `OpenMusE_T1.3_Hospitality_Survey_Survey_Email_data_SK.csv`
- `OpenMusE_T1.3_Hospitality_Survey_Survey_Email_data_SK.sav`

The datasets contain responses from hospitality venues in:

- Hungary (HU)
- Slovakia (SK)

Fieldwork period: 23 May 2025 – 06 August 2025  
Survey mode: Online self-completion (Qualtrics)  
Sampling: Quota sampling within NACE Section I (ORBIS-based sampling frame)

---

### Survey Instrument

- `OpenMusE_T1.3_Hospitality_Survey_Survey_Email_v1.0_master.docx`  
  Master English questionnaire.

- `OpenMusE_T1.3_Hospitality_Survey_Survey_Email_v1.0_trans.xlsx`  
  Translated versions (HU and SK).

---

## Scope of the Survey

The survey covers:

- Venue characteristics
- Background music usage and licensing
- Live music events
- Revenue effects
- Perceived economic value of music

The analysis based on these data is reported in **OpenMusE Deliverable D1.3**.
